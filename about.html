About TechBR Victoria

Our mission, vision, and the story of our community

Our Mission

TechBR Victoria exists to connect, support, and empower Brazilian technology professionals in Victoria, BC. We aim to create a thriving network that fosters professional growth, cultural connection, and community integration.

Our Vision

To be the definitive hub for Brazilian tech talent in Victoria, facilitating career advancement, knowledge sharing, and creating a supportive bridge between Brazilian culture and the Canadian tech ecosystem.

Our Story

2020

The Beginning

A small group of Brazilian developers working in Victoria started meeting for coffee to share experiences about working in Canada.

2021

First Official Meetup

As the group grew, we organized our first official meetup with 15 members, creating a WhatsApp group to stay connected.

2022

Growing Community

The community expanded to include professionals from different IT areas, with regular events and the launch of our first website.

2023

Established Network

TechBR Victoria now boasts over 50 members and partnerships with local tech companies, providing mentorship and job opportunities.

Our Core Values

Community

We believe in the power of a supportive community that helps each other succeed.

We embrace new ideas, technologies, and approaches to solving problems.

Diversity

We celebrate our Brazilian heritage while embracing Canadian multiculturalism.

Growth

We are committed to continuous learning and professional development.
